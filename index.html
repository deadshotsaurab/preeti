About Me

A Soul Woven
in Colour & Light

Born in the village of Maldepur, Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, I grew up with a deep bond with nature — sensing the vibrant threads woven through the landscapes around me. Those golden childhood memories still call me back to that dreamlike world where imagination ran free.

I completed my M.F.A. in Creative Painting from the Faculty of Visual Arts, Banaras Hindu University (BHU), and my B.F.A. in Painting from the College of Arts and Crafts, Lucknow University — topping both with the coveted BHU Gold Medal and Chancellor's Medal.

Influenced by René Magritte's surrealism and Raja Ravi Varma's figure work, my art explores inner emotions, human relationships, nature, and the subconscious mind — presented through rich textures, transparent layering, and symbolic imagery.
